Android Apps & Games Security

The avalanche of APPS creates a tidal wave of opportunities for pirates and hackers around the world – and particularly in the BRIC countries of Brazil, Russia, India and China – to hack your APP. In the early days of APP piracy, the damage was pretty much limited to popular games and APPS – and the outcome was mainly lost revenues, when users figured out ways to download paid APPS for free.

Today, we’re entering a new phase of hacking and cracking, targeting F2P APPS – mostly games. APP piracy nowadays is characterized by sophisticated schemes that go far beyond illegal downloads:
- IP theft: Simply put, someone is distributing your APP for free and leaving you out in the cold.
- SDK removal: Your APP or game is not only compromised; critical data related to advertising, analytics and payments has been erased completely from your APP or game leaving you without the means or data to monetize your game or feedback to optimize it.
- Malware infusion: In this scenario your APP’s SDK has been replaced by malicious SDKs designed to ruin user experience and destroy your business model - or worse.
You can fall victim of such activities when the pirates rob you of IAP revenues, strip out advertising and analytics SDKs, muddy your data and degrade the user experience for legitimate players. You are driving your APP business blind - and that’s never a good strategy.
